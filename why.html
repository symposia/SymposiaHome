Symposia

How can we know what's happening in the world?

We can't...

At least not entirely

Where we come from

Symposia is a product born from the information overload that many people face today as dozens of news agencies constantly barraging us with their own news stories and take on a given situation. This leaves us drowning in a sea of so-called “truth” that everyone seems to be peddling, which deepen divisions and undermine our shared sense of what is true and what is false. Symposia hopes to make the current media landscape easier to parse and to lighten the information load on the average interested citizen..

What we're not

Symposia is not an alternative news agency and does not claim to have a new or special take on a given situation. It does not claim to be an objective news source and believes that to be truly “objective” on a situation is impossible and is both a wasted effort which is not necessarily useful. We make no claims or allegations about any topic or story that comes up and we don't pick any sides.

In order to make informed decisions, citizens need the ability to at least understand issues from a bird’s eye view from all sides of a given issue.

What we're trying to do

The goal of Symposia is neither censorship nor to peddle specific viewpoints, but to hold each outlook and viewpoint accountable on equal footing and put their opposition in conversation with each other — a symposium of sorts on a given news topic. By bringing together a broad and diverse range of opinion based on verified facts, we see Symposia as defining an important new direction on how news information is consumed, and in many ways, a new beginning.
