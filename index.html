Insights of what you read, right where you need them

It can be tough to research a news story in its entirety. With constant incoming articles from hundreds of news sources, it’s impossible for the informed citizen to keep up with a news story while keeping historical context, complex political situations, and an endless barrage of uninformed opinions in mind.

Symposia gathers hundreds of articles from the top news sources in one place for you to explore. Important concepts, perspectives, related media are highlighted for each of these articles, allowing for greater insight and a chance to access a wider perspective that would be difficult to see otherwise. Get historical context and related concepts to any developing story for greater understanding.

See the Big Picture and Hone In

Keeping in mind all the important events/people/places that are related to a given news story is tough, and its hard to see which ideas are the most important in a given story.

Symposia gives you the rough “Lay of the Land” and see how all the important people/places/events are related to this current story. We identify important clusters of articles from these concepts for greater exploration of various perspectives.

Alternatively make your own clusters and hone into the concepts that are important to you. Retrieve articles that address these concepts and hone in on a wide variety of perspectives and narratives.

Get Different Perspectives and Branch Out

Branching out and reading articles that you may not agree with can be tough. Often times its hard to find articles that offer different perspectives and ideas from what you already know. We hope you come to Symposia with an open mind and a willingness to learn more about different perspectives on the most important issues facing our world today.

Symposia lets you filter articles from date, source, related concepts, and similar sentiments to research a certain perspective for a news story. Expand your own perspectives by finding articles that go against the ones you already agree with. We provide similar and different perspective recommendations for any given article for you to explore the story in richer depth and complexity.
